RE-WIND: WIND TURBINE BLADES
AS INFRASTRUCTURE

-or-

the Rewind Network
Years: 2022-2025

Working as an architectural researcher for a multidisciplinary team at Tech developing decommissioned wind turbine blades into bridges, I have completed site analysis and design development for multiple turbine blade centered projects. I led design of new structural proposals for blades as bus stops in Knoxville, TN with hand sketched and digital renders. I surveyed community stakeholders and presented work culminating in a published paper accepted to the 2023 - IC NOCMAT.
